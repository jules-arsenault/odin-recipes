Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 taco sized tortillas
  • pico de gallo
  • 1 handful thinly sliced red onion
  • torn cilantro leaves
  • hotsauce

Steps

  1. in a medium bow, whisk together the 4 eggs
  2. add the chili powder,garlic powder,cumin and kosher salt
  3. whisk until combined
  4. heat olive oil in skillet, add eggs and cook over medium heat
  5. warm and char tortillas
  6. top with pico de gallo,onions, cilantro and hot sauce
